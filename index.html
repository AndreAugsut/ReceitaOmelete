Simple Omelette recipest

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. this classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • sait, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of buffer or oil
  • Optional fillings cheese, diced vegetables, cookeed meatsm, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffler texture.
  2. heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omellette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omellette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook fo another minute, then slide it ont a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
